Capybara

A Capybara is a semi-aqautic mammel that lives
in the amazon it weights 154lb (66kg) they are also
herbivores mostly eating aqautic plants and grasses.
There life span is approximatly 8 - 10 years, They have
a pig shaped body and a fun fact about them is that
due to there hairs being short they can get sun burned

Howler Monkey

The Howler Monkey is a mammel that is known for
making very horrifying and loud howls to scare away
predators and it uses its howls to communicate with one
another. A howler Monkeys diet consists of Nuts and Fruits
found on trees, There life span is approximatly 15 - 20 years.
They weigh around 17.6lb (8kgs).
